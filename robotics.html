Super Image Resolution

Implemented a denoising autoencoder model using convolutional layers to upscale and restore low-resolution images. Combined perceptual and pixel-based loss functions to achieve sharper image reconstructions. Achieved up to 7 dB improvement in PSNR and significant SSIM gains, proving effective for preprocessing in vision pipelines like surveillance, medical imaging, and robotics perception.
